Fruit Bar

Fun spin on a crumble recipe.

Great way to use berries that are either fresh or frozen. Can use a mix of berries or use only one of your favorites.

Ingredients

Filling

  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoon corn starch
  • 2 cups berries
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 3 tablespoon water
  • dash of vanilla

Bars

  • 2 cups oats
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 1/4 brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 cup butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
  2. Making filling by combining filling ingredients on the stovetop and reduce until jam like consistency.
  3. Reserve 2 cups of bar dough, press remaining into grease 9x13 pan.
  4. Bake 10 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and add jam as first layer and the then add reserved dough on top.
  6. Bake 35-40 minutes until golden.
